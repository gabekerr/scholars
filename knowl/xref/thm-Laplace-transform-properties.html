Theorem 5.6.3. Properties of Laplace Transform.

The Laplace transform \(F(s)\) of \(f(t)\text{,}\) when it is defined, satisfies the following properties:
  1. The Laplace transform is linear.
  2. \(\displaystyle \mathcal{L} \left\{ t f(t) \right\} (s) = - \frac{d}{ds} F(s) \)
  3. \(\displaystyle \mathcal{L} \left\{ f^\prime (t) \right\}(s) = s F(s) - f(0) \)
  4. \(\displaystyle \mathcal{L} \left\{f^{(n)} (t) \right\}(s) = s^n F(s) - \sum_{k = 1}^n s^{n - k } f^{(k - 1)} (0) \)
  5. \(\displaystyle \mathcal{L} \left\{e^{at} f (t) \right\} (s) = F(s - a) \)
