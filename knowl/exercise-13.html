Exercise 1.

The equation \(y = mx + b\) is beaten into every young American as a sort of hazing initiation into society. It is usually thought of as the solution to some sort of problem (like ‘find a line with slope ... etc’). However, from the perspective of equation (1.3.2), it is an implicit equation. Give a function \(\mathbf{F}\) which re-expresses the equation for a line as (1.3.2) and find the general solution \(\mathbf{f}\) as in equation (1.3.3)? Can you give more than one example of \(\mathbf{f}\text{?}\)
