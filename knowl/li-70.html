Item 2. Induction Step.

We define \(k\)-th vector
\begin{equation} \mb{w}_k = \mb{v}_k - \left( \frac{\left\langle \mb{v}_k, \mb{w}_1 \right\rangle}{\left\langle \mb{w}_1 , \mb{w}_1 \right\rangle} \mb{w}_1 + \cdots + \frac{\left\langle \mb{v}_k, \mb{w}_{k - 1} \right\rangle}{\left\langle \mb{w}_{k - 1} , \mb{w}_{k - 1} \right\rangle} \mb{w}_{k - 1} \right). \tag{3.2.2} \end{equation}
