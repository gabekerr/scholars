Item 1. Induction Hypothesis.

Assume we have found non-zero orthogonal vectors
\begin{equation*} \{\mb{w}_1 , \ldots , \mb{w}_{k - 1}\} \end{equation*}
so that
\begin{equation*} \textnormal{span} (\{\mb{w}_1 , \ldots , \mb{w}_{k - 1}\} ) = \textnormal{span} (\{\mb{v}_1 , \ldots , \mb{v}_{k - 1}\}). \end{equation*}
