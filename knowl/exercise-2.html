Exercise 2.

Let \(z = -1 - i\) and \(w = 1 - i\text{.}\)
(a)
Compute \(z + w\text{.}\)
(b)
Compute \(z \cdot w\text{.}\)
(c)
Express \(z\) and \(w\) in the form \(re^{i\theta}\) (i.e. in each case identify \(r\) and \(\theta\)).
(d)
Compute \(z^8\text{.}\) For both the grader and your sake, please do it cleverly.
