Suppose \(\mb{c}_1, \ldots, \mb{c}_n\) are column vectors in \(K^m\text{.}\) They are linearly independent if and only if the matrix
\begin{equation*} A = \left[ \begin{matrix} | \amp | \amp \amp | \\ \mb{c}_1 \amp \mb{c}_2 \amp \cdots \amp \mb{c}_n \\ | \amp | \amp \amp | \end{matrix} \right] \end{equation*}
has no free columns.
