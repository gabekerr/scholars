Step 2

Now assume there is a function \(D_{n - 1} : M_{n - 1, n - 1} (K) \to K\text{.}\) If \(A = (a_{ij})\) is an \(n \times n\)-matrix write \(A_{ij}\) for the \((n - 1) \times (n - 1)\)-matrix obtained by forgetting the \(i\)-th row and \(j\)-th column. The \((i,j)\)-minor, denoted \(M_{ij}\text{,}\) is the \(D_{n - 1} (A_{ij})\text{.}\) The \((i,j)\)-cofactor is simply \(C_{ij} = (-1)^{i + j} M_{ij}\text{.}\)
