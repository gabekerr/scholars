Corollary 5.4.2.

If \(\mb{x}_1, \ldots, \mb{x}_n\) are linearly independent solutions to the homogeneous equation
\begin{equation*} \dot{\mb{x}} = A \mb{x} \end{equation*}
and \(\mb{x}_p\) is a particular solution to
\begin{equation*} \dot{\mb{x}} = A \mb{x} + \mb{f} \end{equation*}
then the general solution is given by
\begin{equation} \mb{x} = \mb{x}_p + C_1 \mb{x}_1 + \cdots + C_n \mb{x}_n .\tag{5.4.2} \end{equation}
